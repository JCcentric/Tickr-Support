Tickr – Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 10, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Tickr ("the App") and its developer, Jared Gilbert, collect, use, and protect your information. By using Tickr, you consent to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

Tickr uses Firebase services to provide functionality, including:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Firebase Firestore / Realtime Database
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Firebase Cloud Storage

Through these services, your data may be stored and processed in accordance with Firebase's privacy policies.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the information collected to:

  • Authenticate users
  • Store user-generated countdown timers
  • Improve app functionality through analytics
  • Ensure security and prevent abuse

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information for marketing purposes. However, information may be stored and processed by Firebase according to their policies.

4. Firebase Privacy Policy

By using this app, you acknowledge that Firebase may process and store data. You can view Firebase's Privacy Policy here: https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

6. Your Responsibilities

Do not use Tickr to store sensitive information, including Controlled Unclassified Information (CUI) or classified data.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Continued use of Tickr after changes constitutes your acceptance.
